## Introduction

**AI Safety Daily** is an automatically generated daily digest covering AI safety, alignment, security, privacy, and governance. It spans adversarial attacks & robustness, model alignment & interpretability, privacy-preserving computation & federated learning, agent security & governance, and safety benchmarks.

Reports are produced in **bilingual format** (Chinese and English). The Chinese edition emphasizes academic review-style narrative, while the English edition is tailored for international readers. Sources include arXiv, company blogs (OpenAI, DeepMind, Google AI), tech media (TechCrunch, The Verge, MIT Technology Review, 36kr, QbitAI), HuggingFace, and GitHub.

### Content Structure

Each daily report includes:

- **Highlights** — Top 2-3 developments of the day
- **Adversarial Attacks & Robustness** — Novel attacks/defenses, robustness theory
- **Model Alignment & Interpretability** — Alignment theory, cognitive biases, reasoning boundaries
- **Privacy Protection & Federated Learning** — Differential privacy, federated learning, entity alignment
- **Agent Security & Governance** — Execution environments, safety evaluation, open-source toolchains
- **Safety Benchmarks** — New benchmarks, evaluation methodologies
- **Looking Forward** — Trends and open problems
